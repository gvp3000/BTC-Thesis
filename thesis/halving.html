Supply & Halvings

Bitcoin's supply schedule, halving impact, and stock-to-flow context.
Content Plan
P2
What goes here: Halving schedule and dates. Supply issuance reduction timeline. Stock-to-flow model context (useful framework, not a price predictor). How supply shocks interact with demand catalysts. Current cycle positioning relative to halving.

Key data: April 2024 halving → 3.125 BTC/block. Next halving ~2028. 93.75% of all BTC already mined. Annual inflation rate now below gold.

Note: Supply is the foundation, not the trade. The trade comes from demand catalysts (liquidity, adoption) hitting a supply-constrained asset.
