Dollar Debasement

Why the dollar loses purchasing power and how BTC captures that loss.

Content Plan
P2
What goes here: Historical purchasing power decline. CPI vs real inflation measures. M2 growth rate vs GDP growth — the gap is debasement. Why every fiat currency eventually debases to zero. How BTC acts as the exit valve for this structural process.

The key argument: The dollar has lost 96%+ of purchasing power since 1913. This is not accidental — it is the mathematical result of deficit spending funded by money creation. BTC is the first globally accessible, non-sovereign, verifiably scarce asset that can capture this flow.
