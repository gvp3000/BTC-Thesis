Sentiment & On-Chain

On-chain metrics dashboard and sentiment indicators for cycle positioning.

Content Plan
P1
What goes here: The 2-of-3 confirmation system: MVRV Z-Score, Monthly RSI, Long-Term Holder Supply changes. Plus: NUPL, SOPR, exchange net flows, stablecoin supply ratio, Fear & Greed index.

Decision framework: Green/yellow/red for each metric. Require 2-of-3 core metrics confirming before major action. This solves the indicator overload problem — too many metrics without clear decision rules.

Data sources: Glassnode, CryptoQuant, Coinglass, alternative.me
