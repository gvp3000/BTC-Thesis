Weekly Log & Journal

Structured weekly review, decision journal, and thought capture.

Content Plan
P1
What goes here: Weekly check-in: what changed, conviction level, action needed. The 90-minute weekly workflow checklist (6 steps: on-chain metrics → liquidity check → macro scan → trader sentiment → portfolio check → log entry).

Thought log: Freeform journal entries for capturing ideas, hunches, and observations between reviews. Timestamped. The conviction trend over time is a powerful self-awareness tool — do you get more confident at exactly the wrong times?

This is the operational heartbeat of the site. Without it, everything else is a reference document rather than a living tool.
