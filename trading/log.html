Trade Log

Entry/exit journal with thesis tagging and P&L tracking.

Content Plan
P2
What goes here: Active trades table: asset, entry price, date, size, thesis tag (Phase A / Phase B / discretionary), current P&L, target, stop. Closed trades with win rate and expectancy.

Per-trade detail: Entry rationale, confirming signals, the plan vs what actually happened. Post-trade review. Running statistics: win rate, average R, max drawdown.

Why this matters: Without a log, you can't measure whether the thesis works as a trading edge. The tagging system connects each trade to the intellectual framework.
